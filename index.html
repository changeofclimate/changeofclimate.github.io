Climate change, or global warming, is the warming of the surface of the Earth. There are many pieces of evidence to support climate change, such as melting glaciers. However, many people doubt that humans are the cause of climate change, and some don't even believe in climate change. This website and video was designed to convice people that climate change does exist.

There a few ways to combat climate change. Here are a few:
1) Put a carbon tax on industries that emit CO2, incentivizing decreased carbon usage

2) Install solar panels on your home

3) Refrain from using gasoline-powered cars

4) Trim your waste, since it garbage in landfills emits methanes
